  <how-to-verify>
    These tests require a real iPad with Safari. Run them in order:

    **Step 1 — Serve the production build:**
    Run `npm run preview` in the project directory. This starts a local preview server (default: http://localhost:4173).

    **Step 2 — Open on iPad Safari:**
    On your iPad, open Safari and navigate to your Mac's local IP address on port 4173 (e.g., http://192.168.x.x:4173). Find your Mac's IP with `ipconfig getifaddr en0` in Terminal.

    **Step 3 — Verify installability (PWA-01):**
    Tap the Share button (box with arrow) in Safari. Confirm "Add to Home Screen" appears in the share sheet.
    Tap "Add to Home Screen". Confirm the app name shows as "Math Time" (no exclamation mark — from short_name per D-09).
    Confirm the icon shows the fox mascot (not a blank square).
    Tap Add.

    **Step 4 — Verify standalone mode (PWA-01):**
    From the iPad home screen, tap the Math Time icon you just added.
    Confirm the app launches WITHOUT Safari's browser chrome (no address bar, no tab bar at the top).
    Confirm the app fills the full screen.

    **Step 5 — Trigger first load cache (PWA-02):**
    With the app open in standalone mode and the device still online, navigate through at least one screen (tap the app, go to a lesson if available, return home). This gives the service worker time to precache all assets.
    Wait 5 seconds for the SW install to complete.

    **Step 6 — Verify offline mode (PWA-02):**
    Enable Airplane Mode on the iPad (Control Center → Airplane Mode on).
    Close the Math Time app completely (swipe up from the bottom, swipe the app away).
    Reopen Math Time from the home screen icon.
    Confirm the app loads fully with no network error screen, no "Cannot connect to server" message.
    Navigate to the home screen and confirm lesson cards are visible.
    Confirm no features show error states or loading spinners due to being offline.

    **Step 7 — Verify Safari Manifest Inspector (optional but recommended):**
    While still online, open Safari Developer Tools (requires enabling Web Inspector in iPad Settings → Safari → Advanced → Web Inspector). Connect to Mac via USB. In Safari on Mac, open Develop → [your iPad] → Math Time. In the Elements panel, check that a `<link rel="manifest">` tag is present in `<head>`.
  </how-to-verify>
